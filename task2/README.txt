MULTI SEND SMART CONTRACT


Project Purpose

The Multi-Send Smart Contract allows a sender to send equal amounts of Ether to multiple recipients in a single transaction.

This project is useful for:

1.)Paying salaries to a team

2.)Distributing rewards to users

3.)Saving time by batching transactions


Benefits

Efficiency – Send to many addresses at a time (instead of sending one by one).

Cost-effective – Reduces costs.

Secure – Transactions are verifiable and encrypted.

Automation-ready – Ideal for automatically paying batchwise payments.
